About

Giggles ECCD is committed to providing a nurturing environment where children feel valued and safe. Our center is designed to encourage curiosity, exploration, and learning through structured play and engaging activities. We believe that every child is unique and deserves a tailored learning experience.

Our experienced facilitators focus on developing key skills in communication, problem-solving, creativity, and social interaction. We strive to create a foundation for lifelong learning and success.

Facilities at the Center

  • Facilitator-led activities and child-chosen activities.
  • No screen time at the center.
  • Learning areas include Math, Reading & Literacy, Role-Play, Blocks & Construction, Creative Arts, Games & Puzzle, Science, Sand & Water.
  • Potty training not mandatory.
  • Healthy & nutritious evening snacks provided by the center.
  • ECCD trained facilitators and assistants with adequate teacher-to-child ratio.
  • CCTV surveillance throughout the premises.

Terms & Conditions

We request parents/guardians to familiarize themselves with the center before enrolling their child. A brief orientation session will be conducted to help you understand our policies, activities, and expectations.

In the event of unforeseen circumstances (natural disaster, pandemic, etc.), the center reserves the right to modify operating hours and safety protocols.

All personal information shared with the center will be kept confidential and used only for the well-being and development of the child.

Important Notes

  • Saturday-Sunday/Government Holidays – OFF.
  • Parents/Guardians are requested to familiarize themselves with the center before enrollment.
  • Enrollment may be terminated by either party if deemed unbefitting.
  • Any important conditions regarding your child should be informed to the center beforehand.

Health & Safety

The health and safety of children are our top priorities. Our center follows strict hygiene protocols, and regular sanitization of all learning areas is conducted. Children are monitored for any signs of illness, and immediate action is taken if needed.

Emergency response procedures are in place, and staff are trained to handle any medical or safety emergencies.
