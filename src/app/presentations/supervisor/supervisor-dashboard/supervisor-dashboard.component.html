How to manage surveys:

  1. Click on "Go to Surveys" to view all active surveys assigned to you
  2. Select a survey to see details and enumeration areas
  3. Download CSV templates for each enumeration area
  4. Fill in the household data offline following the template format
  5. Upload the completed CSV files through the Data Upload tab
  6. Track your progress and monitor submission statistics
